Tour De’Himalaya

Among several activities Mountain biking is one of potential sports adventure tourism in Nepal. Mountain biking activities has been limited or concentrated in some selected destinations like Annapurna region and around major city areas. There are so many other unexplored mountain biking areas in Nepal. Newer destinations are required to identify, develop and market it to attract more tourists in coming days. Concept Nepal has been established as non-for-profit organization with its objective to explore new tourists' destination and promote tourism products in coordination with National tourism institution, private tourism organization, local government and local communities. The organization is engaged actively identifying newer destination by exploration, publication of tourism promotional materials like maps, brochure, media campaigning etc. Tour De’ Himalaya is another continuation of it’s works, which is going to explore soon…

The Tour De’ Himalaya is a multiple days international bicycle event primarily held in the lap of Nepal Himalaya, while also occasionally passing through east to west, diverse land and the high passes.

Tips For Tour

ट्राभल टिप्स​

– मुस्ताङ चिसो ठाउँ भएकाले न्याना कपडा, माकल टोपी, पानी बोतल, क्यामेरा, सन ग्लास, कोल्ड क्रिम आदि साथमा बोक्ने । – ठिनी उचाईमा रहेको गाउँ भएकाले सुस्तरी हिँड्ने । बेलाबेलामा पानी पिउने । झोलिलो कुरा बढी पिउने । होमस्टे तथा होटलको परिकारमा रमाउने, परम्परागत लोकल खानेकुरा चाख्ने । – होमस्टेमा बस्ने गरी जाँदा घाम नअस्ताउँदै गाउँमा पुग्ने । तरकारी केलाउन, पकाउन वा अन्य घरायसी काममा होस्ट परिवारलाई सघाउने । राती अबेरसम्म बसेर होहल्ला नगर्ने । – स्थानीय कला, संस्कृति र जीवनशैलीको सम्मान गर्ने । – ठिनी वर्षैभरि घुम्न सकिन्छ । जानु अघि त्यहाँको मौसमको आबश्यक जानकारी लिने ।
